Crudo Chileno

Description

This Chilean raw recipe is inherited from German immigrants in Chile, it is also known as German steak and it is a preparation made with raw beef and lemon. Generally, it is preferred to use lean meat, preferably black or pink beef. This Chilean raw recipe is inherited from German immigrants in Chile, it is also known as German steak and it is a preparation made with raw beef and lemon. In general, it is preferred to use lean meat, preferably black or pink meat.

Ingredients

  • bovine
  • mustard
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • green pepper
  • Fine Salt

Steps

  1. Ablanda la carne amasándola un poco y espárcerla en un bol.
  2. Wash the onion and parsley, then cut them as small as you can.
  3. Add all the ingredients to the meat and mix them well together with enveloping movements, you can do it with a spoon or by kneading.
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour or more. To serve, shape the meat with a cup or tartar. Serve with some toast or crackers and enjoy your Chilean raw food!
